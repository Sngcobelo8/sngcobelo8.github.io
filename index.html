About Us

Founders Story

NSH Youth Empowerment mission is to empower youth (age 18 - 35 years) to enhance health, end hunger, and overcome hardship to care for the communities at large, including taking care of orphaned kids due to HIV/Aids or Cancer impact. Our Vision NSH envisions the world in which the most vulnerable people will have the power to lift themselves out of poverty and to create vital, healthy lives for their families and communities now, and for the future.
